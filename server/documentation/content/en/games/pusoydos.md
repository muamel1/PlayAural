# Pusoy Dos (Big Two)

## Introduction
Pusoy Dos (Big Two) is a popular shedding-type card game. The objective is to be the first player to get rid of all your cards.

## Rules
- The game is played with a standard 52-card deck. Each player is dealt 13 cards.
- **Card Rankings:** The number 2 is the highest card, followed by Ace, King, Queen, Jack, 10, 9, 8, 7, 6, 5, 4, and 3 is the lowest.
- **Suit Rankings:** Diamonds (highest) > Hearts > Spades > Clubs (lowest).
- **First Turn:** The player holding the 3 of Clubs starts the first trick, and must include it in their first play.

## Gameplay
- Players take turns playing card combinations. The combination played must beat the previous combination and be of the same type.
- The types of combinations are:
  - **Single:** A single card.
  - **Pair:** Two cards of the same rank.
  - **Three of a Kind:** Three cards of the same rank.
  - **Five-Card Hand:** Includes Straights, Flushes, Full Houses, Four of a Kind, and Straight Flushes.
- A trick ends when all other players pass consecutively. The last player to play cards starts a new trick with any combination.

## Scoring
- The first player to shed all their cards wins the round.
- The losing players pay the winner a penalty based on the number of cards remaining in their hand, multiplied by the penalty setting.
- If a player holds all 13 cards when the round ends, their penalty is doubled.

## Keybinds
- Space: Play Selected Cards
- p: Pass
- c: Check current trick
- h: Read your hand
- shift+t: Turn timer
