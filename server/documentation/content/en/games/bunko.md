\*\*Bunko\*\*

Bunko is a fast dice game about chasing the round's target number. PlayAural uses a single-table adaptation: everyone plays at one table, rounds rotate through target numbers, and the final winner is decided by the host's selected scoring mode.

\*\*Gameplay\*\*

\* Bunko uses \*\*three six-sided dice\*\*.
\* The default match has \*\*6 rounds\*\*. The host may choose fewer or more rounds.
\* Each round has a \*\*target number\*\*. Round 1 targets 1s, round 2 targets 2s, and so on through 6. If the match has more than 6 rounds, the target numbers cycle back to 1.
\* On your turn, roll all three dice.
\* If the roll scores any points, you keep the dice and roll again.
\* If the roll scores 0, your turn ends and play passes to the next player.

\*\*Special Rolls\*\*

\* \*\*Bunko:\*\* all three dice match the current target number. This scores \*\*21 points\*\* and ends the round immediately.
\* \*\*Mini Bunko:\*\* all three dice match each other, but they do not match the current target number. This scores \*\*5 points\*\* and your turn continues.
\* A normal scoring roll gives \*\*1 point\*\* for each die that matches the target number.

\*\*Round Flow\*\*

\* A round ends when a player reaches or passes \*\*21 points\*\* in that round.
\* A Bunko ends the round immediately because it is worth 21 points.
\* The round winner gains 1 round win.
\* The player sitting after the round winner starts the next round.

\*\*Winning The Match\*\*

The host chooses one of two winning modes:

\* \*\*Round Wins\*\* \(default\): players are ranked by rounds won, then total score, then Bunko count, then mini Bunko count.
\* \*\*Total Score\*\*: players are ranked by total score, then rounds won, then Bunko count, then mini Bunko count.

\*\*Accessibility Notes\*\*

\* The global \*\*Brief announcements\*\* preference shortens frequent roll and round messages while keeping the dice, points, round score, and total score.
\* \*\*Confirm risky actions\*\* is not used in Bunko because rolling is mandatory for normal play and there is no bank/pass decision that could accidentally sacrifice points.

\*\*Customizable Options\*\*

\* \*\*Rounds:\*\* Default 6. Range 1 to 12.
\* \*\*Winning Mode:\*\* Default Round Wins. Choices: Round Wins or Total Score.

\*\*Keyboard Shortcuts\*\*

\* \*\*R:\*\* Roll the dice.
\* \*\*E:\*\* Hear the current round, target number, and leader.
\* \*\*D:\*\* Hear the latest roll and its result.
\* \*\*T:\*\* Check whose turn it is.
\* \*\*S:\*\* Check the standings.
\* \*\*Shift+S:\*\* Open detailed standings.
\* \*\*Ctrl+U:\*\* Hear who is at the table.
