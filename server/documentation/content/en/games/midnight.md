\*\*Midnight (1-4-24)\*\*

Midnight, also known as 1-4-24, is a classic six-dice push-your-luck game. Each player tries to finish a turn with both a 1 and a 4. If you qualify, those two dice are set aside and the other four dice become your score. The best possible turn is 24.

\*\*Gameplay\*\*

A game is played across a fixed number of rounds. In each round, every active player takes one complete dice turn. The qualified player with the highest score wins the round.

\*\*Your Turn\*\*

1\. Roll all six dice.

2\. After every roll, keep at least one newly rolled die. You may keep more than one die if it helps your plan.

3\. Kept dice become locked when you roll again. Locked dice cannot be changed.

4\. Continue until all dice are locked. If all dice are already decided, you may bank the turn instead of rolling again.

\*\*Qualifying\*\*

To score, your final dice must include at least one \*\*1\*\* and at least one \*\*4\*\*. One 1 and one 4 are removed from the score. Any extra 1s or 4s still count as part of the four scoring dice.

If you finish without both required numbers, you do not qualify and score 0 for the round.

\*\*Scoring and Winning\*\*

\* \*\*Turn score:\*\* If you qualify, add the four non-required dice. Maximum score: 24.

\* \*\*Round win:\*\* The single qualified player with the highest score wins the round.

\* \*\*Tied round:\*\* If multiple qualified players tie for the highest score, no round win is awarded.

\* \*\*No qualifiers:\*\* If nobody qualifies, no round win is awarded.

\* \*\*Game win:\*\* After the configured number of rounds, the player with the most round wins wins the game. If players are still tied, the game ends in a tie.

\*\*Status Checks\*\*

\* \*\*Read current dice:\*\* Reads the current player's dice, including which dice are locked or kept, what is still live, and whether the current dice already qualify.

\* \*\*View round status:\*\* Opens a live status panel with the round number, current turn, last completed turn, and standings.

\* \*\*Scores:\*\* Reads each player's round wins. Detailed scores open a live standings panel.

\*\*Example Turn\*\*

1\. You roll: \*\*3-1-5-4-2-6\*\*.

2\. You keep the 1 and 4 to qualify, and also keep the 6.

3\. You roll the remaining three dice: \*\*2-5-3\*\*. You keep the 5.

4\. You roll the final two dice: \*\*6-6\*\*. The turn is complete.

5\. Your final dice are 1, 4, 6, 5, 6, and 6. The 1 and 4 qualify you; the score is 6 + 5 + 6 + 6 = 23.

\*\*Game Options\*\*

\* \*\*Rounds:\*\* Number of rounds to play. Default: 5. Range: 1-20.

\*\*Keyboard Shortcuts\*\*

\* \*\*R:\*\* Roll the dice.

\* \*\*1-6:\*\* Toggle keeping a die, using your personal dice-keeping style.

\* \*\*B:\*\* Bank your score once every die is decided.

\* \*\*H:\*\* Read the current dice.

\* \*\*E:\*\* View round status.

\* \*\*S:\*\* Read scores.

\* \*\*Shift+S:\*\* View detailed scores.
