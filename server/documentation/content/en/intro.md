\*\*Welcome to PlayAural\*\*



Welcome to PlayAural, an online gaming platform designed with an audio-first approach and a strong focus on accessibility. Whether you are a screen reader user, a casual player, or someone discovering accessible gaming for the first time, PlayAural is built so that you can jump straight into the action without any barriers.



Every part of the platform — from menus and game actions to chat and notifications — is designed to be fully navigable by keyboard and screen reader. All game events are announced through synthesized speech and spatial audio cues, so you never have to rely on visual information to understand what is happening.

I am Trung (ddt.one), the developer and administrator of this version of PlayAural.

PlayAural is available on two platforms:



\* \*\*Desktop Client:\*\* A dedicated application for Windows, built with accessibility in mind. It integrates deeply with screen readers and offers the full range of keyboard shortcuts.

\* \*\*Web Client:\*\* A browser-based Progressive Web App (PWA) that works on any device, including mobile phones and tablets. It supports touch navigation through the Shortcuts tab for users without a physical keyboard.

\*\*Getting Started\*\*



1\. \*\*Play:\*\* Select "Play" from the Main Menu to browse the game catalog. From there, you can create a new table or view existing ones for any specific game.

2\. \*\*Active Tables:\*\* Located below the "Play" button, this option lets you see all tables currently active on the server across every game, so you can quickly find a game already in progress to join.

3\. \*\*Friends:\*\* Open the Friends Hub from the Main Menu (or press Alt+F on the desktop client, or use the Shortcuts tab on mobile) to manage your friends list, send friend requests, and send private messages.

4\. \*\*Options:\*\* Open the Options menu (or press Alt+O) to customize your language, speech voice, speech rate, dice-keeping style, and other preferences. Changes take effect immediately.

5\. \*\*Invite Players:\*\* Once at a table, you can invite friends or add computer-controlled Bots to fill empty seats.



\*\*Chat Commands\*\*



You can use special commands in the chat box by typing a forward slash `/` followed by a keyword:



\* \*\*/g message\*\* (or \*\*/global\*\*): Send a message to the global chat channel, visible to everyone on the server.



\*\*Friends \& Social Features\*\*



PlayAural includes a social layer so you can stay connected with the people you play with most.



\* \*\*Friends List:\*\* Add other players as friends. Your friends list shows who is online, what game they are playing, and whether they are available to join.

\* \*\*Private Messages:\*\* Send a private message directly to any friend, even while you are in the middle of a game.

\* \*\*Friend Requests:\*\* Send and accept friend requests from the Friends Hub. You will be notified when someone accepts your request.

\* \*\*Public Profiles:\*\* View another player's public profile from the online users list or from your friends list to see their username and other public information.

\* \*\*Online Users:\*\* Press Shift+F2 (or open from the Main Menu) to see who is currently online and what they are up to.

\* \*\*Scopa:\*\* A traditional Italian card game of capturing and strategy.

\* \*\*Pirates of the Lost Seas:\*\* A competitive RPG adventure. Sail the seas, battle rivals, and collect gems to claim victory.
