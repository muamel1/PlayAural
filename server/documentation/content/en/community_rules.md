\*\*Community Rules\*\*



PlayAural aims to create a fun, equal, and accessible gaming space for everyone. To maintain this environment, please follow these guidelines. By playing on PlayAural, you agree to abide by these rules.



\*\*1. Multilingual Environment\*\*



PlayAural welcomes players from all over the world.



\* \*\*No Language Restrictions:\*\* We do not ban or restrict any specific language in the global chat. You are free to speak your native language.

\* \*\*Communication:\*\* As this is a diverse community, we encourage players to use translation tools to understand each other when necessary.

\* \*\*Personal Preference:\*\* If you find the global chat distracting or prefer not to see other languages, you can disable Global Chat in your Options at any time.



\*\*2. Conduct \& Behavior\*\*



\* \*\*Respect Others:\*\* Treat every player with courtesy and respect. Harassment, personal insults, hate speech, discrimination, or targeted hostility of any kind will not be tolerated.

\* \*\*No Spam:\*\* Avoid flooding the chat with repeated messages, unsolicited advertisements, or excessive commands that disrupt the experience for others.

\* \*\*No Offensive Content:\*\* Do not use obscene, sexually explicit, or deeply offensive language in public chats. Private messages must also respect the dignity of the recipient.

\* \*\*Threats \& Intimidation:\*\* Any form of real-world threats, doxing, or deliberate intimidation targeting another player is strictly prohibited and may result in immediate removal.



\*\*3. Fair Play\*\*



PlayAural is built for everyone to enjoy on equal footing. Please help keep the competition fair.



\* \*\*No Cheating or Exploiting:\*\* Using external software, scripts, or intentionally exploiting bugs or unintended game mechanics to gain an unfair advantage is not allowed. If you discover a bug, please report it rather than abuse it.

\* \*\*No Stalling:\*\* Deliberately taking excessive time on your turn to annoy opponents or time out a session is considered unsportsmanlike behavior.

\* \*\*No Intentional Disconnection:\*\* Leaving a game mid-session to avoid a loss is disrespectful to other players. Disconnections happen — but making a habit of it will be noticed.

\* \*\*Account Integrity:\*\* Do not share accounts or use multiple accounts to manipulate rankings or statistics. Each person should play under a single, honest identity.



\*\*4. Privacy \& Private Messages\*\*



\* \*\*Respect Privacy:\*\* Do not share another player's private messages, personal information, or in-game communications publicly without their explicit consent.

\* \*\*Private Message Conduct:\*\* The rules on respectful behavior apply equally to private messages. Using private messages to harass or threaten another player is not acceptable.



\*\*5. Game Etiquette\*\*



\* \*\*Be a Good Sport:\*\* Win or lose, treat your fellow players with grace. Excessive taunting after a win or angry outbursts after a loss detract from everyone's enjoyment.

\* \*\*Bots \& AI Players:\*\* Bot players are provided to fill empty seats and keep games moving. Do not attempt to exploit bot behavior to gain unfair advantages.

\* \*\*Help New Players:\*\* If you see a newcomer struggling, a word of encouragement or a brief tip goes a long way toward building a welcoming community.



\*\*6. Administrative Discretion\*\*



To ensure a safe and enjoyable environment for the community:



\* The administrator reserves the right to issue warnings, mute, temporarily suspend, or permanently ban any account found to be in serious or repeated violation of these rules, without prior notice.

\* Decisions regarding enforcement are made at the administrator's discretion. If you believe an action was taken in error, you are welcome to reach out respectfully to discuss it.



\*\*7. Updates \& Feedback\*\*



\* \*\*Work in Progress:\*\* As PlayAural is currently in active development, these rules are subject to change and may be updated at any time to better serve the community.

\* \*\*Feedback Welcome:\*\* Your suggestions regarding game rules, features, or community guidelines are highly appreciated. Please feel free to share your thoughts through the in-game feedback options or by contacting the administrator directly.



Thank you for being part of PlayAural and helping make it a great place for everyone!
